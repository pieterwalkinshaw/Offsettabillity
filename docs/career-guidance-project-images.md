# Image Generation Prompts: Pathways to Purpose

Use these prompts with Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion to generate compelling visuals for the Career Guidance project.

---

## 1. Hero Image — The Assessment Moment

**Prompt:**
> A 15-year-old Black South African girl in school uniform sitting at a desk in a rural school classroom, focused and engaged while completing a psychometric assessment on a tablet device. A friendly female psychometrist in professional attire sits beside her, guiding her through the process. Warm natural light streams through the window. The classroom is simple but clean. Other students visible in the background waiting their turn. Hopeful, empowering atmosphere. Documentary photography style, warm color palette, shallow depth of field. 4K, photorealistic.

**Usage:** Project hero/banner image, project detail page header

---

## 2. Before & After — The Blind Choice

**Prompt:**

LEFT SIDE: A confused Black South African teenager in school uniform staring at a blank subject selection form, looking overwhelmed and uncertain, grey/muted tones, empty classroom. ## Brand Guidelines for Images

- **Color palette:** Primary green (#16a34a), gold accents (#d97706), warm neutrals
- **Tone:** Hopeful, empowering, dignified (never pitying or patronizing)
- **Subjects:** Always show agency and engagement, never passive recipients
- **Setting:** Authentic South African rural contexts, not generic "Africa" imagery
- **Quality:** High resolution, professional photography style
- **Diversity:** Reflect the actual demographics of Eastern Cape, Limpopo, KZN learners

---

## Usage Rights Note

When generating these images, ensure you have appropriate commercial usage rights for your chosen AI tool. For the most authentic results, consider commissioning a South African documentary photographer to capture real moments from the project's deployment.
